Description:

To Go in Faith is our weekly Friday evangelism sessions, where we initiate spiritual conversations on-grounds at UVA. We engage in active prayer and share the Gospel in obedience to the Great Commission, giving others the opportunity to hear, consider, and respond to the good news of Jesus Christ. At TGIF, we offer a collaborative space to grow and exercise our faith together as a community, equipping you with tools to confidently begin Gospel conversations.

What is Apologetics?

  • Explaining why Christianity is true
  • Defending the faith against objections
  • Strengthening believers’ confidence in the Gospel
  • Engaging non-believers with thoughtful answers

Description:

Apologetics45 is a 45-minutes class session offered to students and believers for a stronger theological foundation. Each class session aims to explore challenging questions that arise against Christianity in an attempt to seek the truth, justify beliefs, and defend the faith. The ultimate goal is to strengthen and prepare Christians for Gospel sharing.

Apologetics45 + 15


Apologetics45 + 15 is a 15-minute evangelism training held directly after each Apologetics45 session. These brief add-ons equip students to confidently use tools like the Perspective Cards, Snapshot, and Spiritual Surveys as part of TGIF outreach.

About

We are followers of Christ who are seeking to fulfill the great commission of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ. We are affiliated with Cru, formerly known as Campus Crusade for Christ (CCC). Magnify is a student-led initiative in partnership with Cru that aims to equip and encourage students to live out their faith boldly and share the Gospel on-grounds. It was created to promote and assist members of the ministry at UVA by organizing events, links, and other resources for better ease when navigating information.

Cru's Mission: Win, build and send Christ-centered multiplying disciples who launch spiritual movements.
Cru's Vision: Movements everywhere so that everyone knows someone who truly follows Jesus.
Values: Faith, Growth and Fruitfulness
